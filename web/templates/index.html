Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about creating stunning infographics with AI

How does the AI understand my content? +

Our advanced AI analyzes your text using natural language processing to identify key themes, extract important points, categorize content types, and understand the overall message. It then creates a visual hierarchy that best represents your content.

Do I need design skills to use this? +

Not at all! The AI handles all the design decisions for you. Just paste your content and get a professional-looking infographic outline instantly. The tool is designed for non-designers who need quick, effective visual content.

What types of content work best? +

Any text content works! Articles, blog posts, meeting notes, research findings, product descriptions, how-to guides, statistics, and more. The AI adapts to different content types and creates appropriate visual representations.

Can I use this for commercial purposes? +

Yes! The infographics you create can be used for any purpose - presentations, social media, marketing materials, reports, websites, and more. The AI-generated content is yours to use commercially.

Is my data secure and private? +

Your content is processed securely and isn't stored permanently. We use industry-standard encryption and don't share your data with third parties. Demo mode processes everything locally without any external API calls.

What platforms are supported? +

The layouts are optimized for all major platforms: presentations (PowerPoint, Google Slides), social media (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram), websites, print materials, and more. Choose your target platform for best results.

Can I export the results? +

Yes! Download your results as JSON data that you can import into design tools, integrate with your applications, or use with other software. The structured data includes all the layout specifications and content elements.

How accurate are the AI-generated images? +

Our AI image generation uses state-of-the-art models to create relevant, high-quality visuals that match your content. While not perfect, they provide excellent starting points for your infographics and can be refined as needed.
